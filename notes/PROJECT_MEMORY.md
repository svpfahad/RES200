## Research Positioning

Do not frame QLattice as outperforming XGBoost based on current evidence. The stronger angle is interpretable symbolic pKa modeling: QLattice can produce compact formulas and chemically inspectable feature interactions, while XGBoost remains the accuracy benchmark.

For a state-of-the-art paper, the defensible contribution should be: curated acidic/basic pKa benchmark splits, strong modern baselines, QLattice symbolic models trained under leakage-safe feature selection, interpretability analysis of selected formulas, and honest performance/complexity tradeoffs.

## Paper Direction

Working paper concept: `Interpretable Symbolic Regression for Molecular pKa Prediction Using QLattice-Guided Descriptor Discovery`.

Core story: accurate pKa prediction matters, black-box models work well but are hard to interpret, QLattice offers symbolic equations that expose descriptor relationships, and the study quantifies the accuracy-interpretability tradeoff against strong cheminformatics baselines.

The paper should include: dataset curation, descriptor generation, leakage-safe feature filtering, baseline model setup, QLattice symbolic search, benchmark table, formula complexity table, predicted-vs-experimental plots, residual plots, applicability domain, chemical interpretation of formulas, and limitations.
