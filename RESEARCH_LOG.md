## Hypotheses

- **H1** [confirmed]: Leakage-safe QLattice produces compact pKa formulas (~8–12 descriptors) dominated by partial-charge/electronic terms. — *Confirmed: acidic 8 descr R²0.448, basic 12 descr R²0.470; selected descriptors are charge-weighted.*
- **H2** [rejected]: Distilling a LightGBM teacher into a symbolic surrogate beats direct symbolic fitting. — *Rejected: distillation R²0.33–0.35 < direct 0.45–0.47. Dense tree interactions don't compress into a compact formula. Reportable negative result.*
- **H3** [partially rejected / nuanced]: The OP2-trained symbolic formulas generalize to external blind benchmarks. — *Tested on SAT4pKa-curated external sets (Novartis, AvLiLuMoVe, SAMPL7; neutralized to OP2's neutral-structure convention). The symbolic formulas transfer **weak-to-moderate, above-chance rank signal** (Spearman 0.27–0.58 on 4/5 sets vs a y-random floor of 0.07–0.22; pooled acidic ρ=0.58 R²=0.34) — so the encoded electronic structure is not purely OP2-split-specific — but they are **poorly calibrated out-of-distribution** (pooled basic R²=−0.38 despite ρ=0.42; per-set slopes 0.26–0.76) and are **out-ranked by the LightGBM ceiling** (pooled external ρ 0.71/0.82, R² 0.49/0.67; paired Wilcoxon p≈2e-4 acidic, 1e-21 basic — E10). Net: symbolic skill does not match in-distribution accuracy externally; the GBM degrades from R²≈0.84 to 0.40–0.67 but keeps the better external profile. SAMPL6 dropped (no in-repo labels; tiny N). See `sota_pka/runs/external_holdout/RESULTS_external_holdout.md`.*
- **H3b** [new, confirmed]: The symbolic models' OOD failure decomposes into *miscalibration* (recoverable by affine scaling) vs *lost association* (irreducible). — *E7 out-of-fold affine recalibration (`runs/uncertainty_calibration/`): **acidic** symbolic is already well-calibrated externally (pooled R²_raw 0.336 ≈ R²_recal 0.337 ≈ affine ceiling/Pearson² 0.356) — its external deficit is loss of linear association, not scale. **Basic** symbolic's catastrophic R²=−0.38 is **almost entirely miscalibration**: a 2-parameter OOF affine correction recovers it to **+0.165** (≈ ceiling 0.177), with the residual gap reflecting genuinely weak association (Pearson 0.42). The GBM/RF ceilings need no recalibration (R²_raw≈R²_recal). Spearman is affine-invariant throughout. This converts "miscalibrated OOD" into a quantified, publishable diagnosis.*

- **D1** (2026-06-05): Rebuilt QLattice pipeline leakage-safe inside `sota_pka/`; discarded the older notebooks' ~0.54 figure as model-selection leakage (they read test R² to pick the config).
- **D2** (2026-06-12): Adopted the autonomous `/research` loop. The external SAMPL6/7 holdout is the single biggest credibility gap and is the agent's first move; the novelty gate runs only after that evidence exists.
- **D3** (2026-06-13): Replaced the SAMPL6/7-minimal holdout plan with **SAT4pKa-curated external sets** (Novartis acidic/basic, AvLiLuMoVe acidic/basic, SAMPL7_acidic) — SAMPL6 minimal ships no in-repo experimental labels and both SAMPL sets are tiny (N≈20–24) and microstate-structured. SAT4pKa sets are single-value, pre-split acidic/basic (matching the two task models), and are the canonical external benchmarks reported by SAT4pKa/QupKake/MolGpKa/Uni-pKa → results are directly comparable to published SOTA. Driver: `sota_pka/external_holdout.py`; summary: `sota_pka/external_summary.py`.
- **D5** (2026-06-13): **E6 is not contaminated.** Dedup audit (E9) in the neutralized-canonical space E6 scored in finds exact train/external structure overlap only in AvLiLuMoVe_basic (2/97); both Novartis sets and SAMPL7 are clean (0). Re-scoring with overlaps removed (E9b) leaves every conclusion intact and, notably, does **not** raise the symbolic numbers — so the audit was never propped up by leakage. Report the de-duplicated numbers (or footnote the 2-molecule exclusion). Also surfaced: OP2's own acidic train/test split shares 1 molecule — a pre-existing dataset quirk to footnote, immaterial to the leakage-safe pipeline (which selects features train-only).
- **D6** (2026-06-13): **Uncertainty is reported two ways.** Bootstrap 95% CI on the single held-out test R² (E7) + the inner-CV band already agree and bracket the point estimate; the gold-standard nested-CV band (E8) is the confirmatory third leg the novelty gate asked for. Keep all three in the paper — they tell a consistent stability story (test R² is not a lucky split).
- **D4** (2026-06-13): **External SMILES are neutralized** (RDKit Uncharger + largest-fragment parent) before featurization, because OP2 trained on `OriginalSmiles` = the neutral drawn structure while SAT4pKa ships ionized conjugate forms. Scoring ionized inputs against neutral-trained models is invalid; the raw (ionized) run (E6b) quantifies the effect — catastrophic collapse — making "standardize protonation state to the training convention" a reportable benchmarking caveat. The generalization gap is measured *within each re-fit model* (reproduced OP2-test R² anchors the recorded models: acidic 0.448 exact, basic 0.452).
